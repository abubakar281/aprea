Existing Real Estate
Documenting Structure

The documentation matters for real estate industry in Pakistan is majorly dealt by the revenue department. The department has set its organizational structure for processing the transference of ownership rights for properties and several other documentations for the real estate. Similarly, development authorities and private developers follow their own procedures for documenting real estate assets.However, the current documentations are mostly drawn by paper oriented methods,which poses a wide range of issues for the industry to work properly. Pakistan Land Department-
The Way Forward

Pakistan Lnad Department would be a centralized department for the whole country to carry out the registring, documenting, managing and keeping records of all the real estates in the country. The department will operate in preselected key areas of Pakistan while attaining a unified online databank for the entire country.
The working system of PLD will be based advanced technological assets, taking away the complexities of current domentation procedures. The developed real estate data bank under Pakistan Land Department will play key role in ensuring secure and transparent land transfers through a technological oriented structure.

The newly established land department will be responsible for the due security, secrecy and necessary safeguards for protection and confidentiality of data and information contained in the registration and database systems developed by the body. Moreover, PLD will prepare and submit annual reports to the Federal Government in respect of performance and exercise of its functions, powers and its achievements.
