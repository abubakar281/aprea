Pakistan Real Estate Policy

In Pakistan, currently there is no mechanism for licensing the brokers working as the lower tier of the real estate industry. A big reason behind is the absence of a regulatory body for regularizing the working of real estate industry. Selfly developed brokers, coming from diversified backgrounds, are working in the industry, even without having expertise in the said field. This leads towards an unmonitored growth in the brokerage community because anyone can claim to be a real estate broker, resulting in malpractices in the matters related to property in the country. Therefore, it is inevitable to license the said community for securing the rights of brokerageship in the country.

Importance of Real Estate License

Licensing reflects a mechanism through which a legal authorization to a person, willing to work in a certain domain,is provided to make him continue working in a secure environment. This licensing stands beneficial for all the parties involved in the industry. Similarly for the real estate industry in Pakistan, it is necessary to develop a licensing platform for restricting the real estate dealing with the certified professionals. This will ultimately help in building a validated real estate brokerage community because real estate brokers will be coming from adopted standardized procedure.
