Current Real Estate Education in Pakistan

Currrently, Pakistan lacks in having a developed structure for provision of real estate education in the country. There are very few institutes in the country providing education related to real estate and are insufficient to cope up with the population extent, deserving to be provided education in the said field. As a result, real estate brokers lacking in having basic education appers to be dealing with complex real estate transactions. This leads towards hindered transfer processes due to insufficient knowledge and experience of the broker for property dealings. To come up with the situation, there is a severe need to make arrangements for educating the real estate community in the country.

Pakistan Real Estate Institute

Pakistan Real Estate Institute, through it’s platform envisions to launch certain educational initiatives in the country. The way forward for doing this is to build a thriving real estate educational institute for eductaing the real estate community through different means. For this purpose, Pakistan Real Estate Institute is aimed to be introduced in the country, owning a unique identity for being a real estate educational centre operating in different parts of the state. The institute will offer a wide range of real estate programs encompassing varied stakeholders of the industry.
