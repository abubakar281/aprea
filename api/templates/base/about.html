Our Vision

APREA is a trusted ally, licensing all the brokerage community and those who serve through the ever-evolving real estate landscape. It is building a better future for the real estate industry through its commitment to bringing excellence to the brokerage community.

Our Mission

APREA's mission is to educate and empower real estate brokers as they preserve, protect, and advance the right to real estate property for all. It will also uphold the highest standards of ethics and professionalism and protect clients from unlawful practices.

Diversity & Inclusion

APREA fully embraces perspectives from all walks of life and provides licensing to all—regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, religion, age, disability, or socioeconomic status or any other qualities, by which we may define ourselves.

Contributions

APREA, through its platform, aims to serve the real estate industry through certain contributions including brokerage community registration, documenting the real estate industry, educating the stakeholders in the existing real estate market and devising a brief real estate policy in the country to digitally and legally support the current real estate system.

Abdur Raheem Wyne

Founder of APREA

Abdur Raheem Wyne has worked relentlessly for the betterment of the real estate industry of Pakistan and society, especially by creating a Pakistan Real Estate Institute to educate the real estate community. As the founder of All Pakistan Real Estate Associations, has been instrumental in fostering transparency by providing license licensing to the brokerage community and membership to all the union, association, and group leaders.
He has spent over 18 years in the field, studying and practicing the real estate structures in Gulf & European countries and how they benefit their respective economies. Therefore, he understands the nuances of the real estate industry and its contributions to the national economy. He is also the CEO of a research based firm Rise Marketing Consultants, a company aiming to resolve the complex real estate through their consultancy services.
Raheem Wyne strongly believes that Pakistan's real estate market can play a vital role in the financial challenges of Pakistan's government. Therefore, a platform was required to gather the community leaders to contribute to reforms in Real estate national policy

Code of Ethics

APREA is a platform that introduces a code of ethics for the real estate industry, making it simple and easy to follow.
As a real estate agent, you know how important it is to be ethical. You understand how your reputation impacts everything from repeat business to client referrals—and even your ability to get hired in the first place. That's why APREA exists: so that you can focus on doing what you do best.

APREA’s Strategic Action Plan

Membership

All the union leaders, associations, and group leaders will be considered members of All Pakistan Real Estate Associations. They can further make necessary efforts to promote licensing for the brokers working within their territory endorsed and issues by APREA. They can participate in all conferences and seminars held under APREA’s platform.

Learn more

License

APREA’s main purpose is to bring all the brokerage communities represented by their respective leaders under one platform to license them, which will directly affect the real estate industry. Furthermore, APREA will organize, educate and bring transparency to the brokerage community across Pakistan.
