Empower your Brokerage Community

The brokerage community is an integral part of the real estate industry. The industry's expansion and development over the last several decades have been largely driven by a handful of powerful developers. Therefore, APREA provides a platform for all the union leaders, group leaders, and other individual real estate agents to come together under one unified platform to empower the real estate industry.

APREA's goal is to bring together people who are committed to reforming the 400 years old system into the land record management system (LRMS) of the 21st century. The main agenda is to organize, license, educate, and bring transparency within the brokerage community as brokers are the second most pillar of the real estate industry.
