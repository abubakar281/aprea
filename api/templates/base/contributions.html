Registering
the Brokers

In Pakistan, currently there is no mechanism for licensing the brokers working as the lower tier of the real estate industry. A big reason behind is the absence of a regulatory body for regularizing the working of real estate industry. Selfly developed brokers, coming from diversified backgrounds, are working in the industry, even without having expertise in the said field.

Documenting
Real Estate Industry

The documentation matters for real estate industry in Pakistan is majorly dealt by the revenue department. The department has set its organizational structure for processing the transference of ownership rights for properties and several other documentations for the real estate.

Educating
Stakeholders

Currrently, Pakistan lacks in having a developed structure for provision of real estate education in the country. There are very few institutes in the country providing education related to real estate and are insufficient to cope up with the population extent, deserving to be provided education in the said field.

Existing Brokerage Model in Pakistan

Currently, there is no licensing system for real estate brokerage community in Pakistan.
Brokers work independently without any monitoring or regulations.
This lead to uncontrolled extent of brokerage community because any one can become one.
